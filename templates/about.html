About Greenfield Local Hub

We're a cooperative of local farmers and food producers, working together to bring fresh, sustainable, and traceable products to our community.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable local food system that benefits farmers, producers, consumers, and the environment. We believe in transparency, quality, and keeping our food system local and traceable.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible farming and production methods that protect our land for future generations.

Community

Supporting local farmers and producers means investing in our community's economic health and social fabric.

Quality

Every product meets our strict quality standards. We guarantee freshness, flavor, and nutritional value.

What We Offer

Retail Outlet

A single location where you can find products from all our local producers. Visit us in person or shop online for delivery or collection.

Collective Marketing

We promote our producers together, giving small farms and artisan makers the visibility they deserve in a competitive market.

Product Management

Advanced stock control and inventory management help our producers focus on what they do best - making great food.

Full Traceability

Know exactly where your food comes from. Every product can be traced back to its producer and production method.

Our Story

Greenfield Local Hub was founded in 2020 by a group of local farmers who saw the challenges facing small-scale food producers. Rising costs, limited market access, and competition from large supermarkets made it difficult to sustain their businesses.

By working together as a cooperative, we've created a platform that benefits everyone involved. Producers gain access to a wider market and shared resources, while customers enjoy fresh, local products with complete transparency about their origins.

Today, we work with over 20 local producers across Greenfield and serve hundreds of families who value quality, sustainability, and community. We're proud to be part of the local food movement and excited about the future.
